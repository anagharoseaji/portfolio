Fallen Angel

Cyanotype, Applique | 7x4 ft' | 2025

This work explores the tension between attraction and threat, through the metaphor of vibrant colour, hues that appear beautiful, yet conceal an underlying sense of danger.

The visuals mirror the curiosity and innocence with which one steps into unfamiliar spaces, attempting to understand, adapt, and consciously blend into new environments.

While this process of exploration holds possibility, it is shadowed by the persistence of past memories. These fragments linger like echoes, haunting the present and shaping perception.
